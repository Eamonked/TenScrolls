# Ten Scrolls Widgets - User Guide

## Overview

Ten Scrolls offers **two widgets** that you can add to your iPhone home screen or lock screen:

1. **Daily Practice Widget** - Shows your streak and today's reading progress
2. **Journal Reflection Widget** - Displays random journal entries throughout the day

---

## 📱 How to Add Widgets to Your Home Screen

### Step 1: Enter Edit Mode
- **Long press** on an empty area of your home screen
- Wait for the apps to start wiggling

### Step 2: Open Widget Gallery
- Tap the **"+"** button in the top-left corner
- Or tap "Edit Home Screen" if that option appears

### Step 3: Find Ten Scrolls
- Scroll through the widget list to find **"Ten Scrolls"**
- Or use the search bar at the top and type "Ten Scrolls"

### Step 4: Choose Your Widget
You'll see two widgets to choose from:

#### Daily Practice Widget
- Shows your current streak 🔥
- Displays today's reading stamps (dawn ☀️, midday ☀️, dusk 🌙)
- Shows active scroll title and progress

#### Journal Reflection Widget
- Displays one random journal entry at a time
- Rotates automatically every 2 hours
- Shows entry date and scroll reference

### Step 5: Select Size
Each widget comes in different sizes:

**Daily Practice Widget:**
- **Small** (2×2) - Streak + 3 stamps
- **Medium** (4×2) - Scroll title + streak + stamps
- **Circular** (Lock screen) - Just your streak number
- **Rectangular** (Lock screen) - Mini version with all info

**Journal Reflection Widget:**
- **Small** (2×2) - Short excerpt, up to 5 lines
- **Medium** (4×2) - Comfortable reading, up to 4 lines  
- **Large** (4×4) - Full display, up to 12 lines
- **Rectangular** (Lock screen) - Tiny preview, 2 lines

### Step 6: Add to Home Screen
- Swipe through the preview to see different sizes
- Tap **"Add Widget"** when you've chosen your preferred size
- Drag to position it where you want on your home screen
- Tap **"Done"** in the top-right corner

---

## 🔒 How to Add Widgets to Your Lock Screen (iOS 16+)

### Step 1: Enter Lock Screen Edit Mode
- **Long press** on your lock screen (when locked or from notifications)
- Tap **"Customize"** or **"+"** to create a new lock screen

### Step 2: Add Widgets
- Tap on the widget area (below the time or above the bottom)
- Tap the **"+"** or widget placeholder area

### Step 3: Choose Ten Scrolls
- Find **"Ten Scrolls"** in the widget list
- Choose between:
  - **Circular** - Your streak in a circle (goes in the row of circles below the time)
  - **Rectangular** - Progress info (goes in the area above the bottom)

### Step 4: Position and Save
- Place the widget where you want it
- Tap **"Done"** to save your lock screen

## 📖 Using the Journal Reflection Widget

### What It Does
The Journal Reflection widget shows you **one random journal entry at a time**, helping you:
- Rediscover past insights
- Stay motivated by remembering your journey
- Reflect on your progress
- Get inspired throughout the day

### How It Works

#### Automatic Rotation
- Widget picks a **different entry every 2 hours**
- You don't need to do anything - it updates automatically
- Each entry shows:
  - The journal text
  - Date it was written
  - Which scroll you were on

#### What You'll See

**If you have journal entries:**
- A random entry from your journal
- "JOURNAL" or "JOURNAL REFLECTION" header (depending on size)
- Date (e.g., "Dec 15")
- Scroll reference (e.g., "Scroll IV")
- Your journal text (amount depends on widget size)

**If you don't have entries yet:**
- Book icon 📖
- "No journal entries yet" message
- Prompt to start journaling

#### Which Entries Appear?
- Only **published** journal entries (not drafts)
- Up to your 50 most recent entries
- Rotates through all entries before repeating

### Best Practices

**Widget Size Recommendations:**

- **Small (2×2)**: Quick glances, short quotes
- **Medium (4×2)**: Best balance of space and readability ⭐ Recommended
- **Large (4×4)**: For longer reflections, most immersive
- **Lock Screen**: Quick reminder without unlocking phone

**Tips:**
- Keep journal entries concise for better widget display
- Longer entries work better in Large widget
- The widget shows the most important parts first (truncates if too long)

### Updating the Widget

#### Manual Refresh
If you want to see a different entry right away:
1. **Long press** on the widget
2. Tap **"Refresh Widget"**
3. A new entry will appear immediately

#### Automatic Updates
The widget automatically refreshes when:
- Every 2 hours (new entry rotation)
- You add or edit a journal entry in the app
- You change your theme in the app

## 🔥 Using the Daily Practice Widget

### What It Shows

**Streak Count** 🔥
- Your current consecutive days of practice
- Updates immediately when you complete a day

**Daily Stamps**
Three circles showing today's reading status:
- ☀️ **Dawn** - Morning reading (5am-11am)
- ☀️ **Midday** - Afternoon reading (11am-4pm)  
- 🌙 **Dusk** - Evening reading (4pm-11pm)

**Scroll Progress** (Medium widget only)
- Title of your active scroll
- Days completed (e.g., "8 / 30 DAYS")
- Scroll roman numeral (e.g., "SCROLL IV")

### How Stamps Work

**Filled Circle** (colored) = Completed ✅
**Empty Circle** (outline) = Not yet done ⭕

Example:
- ●⭕⭕ = Dawn complete, midday and dusk pending
- ●●● = All three readings done for today! 🎉

### Real-Time Updates

The widget updates automatically when:
- You complete a reading session in the app
- Your streak increases
- You move to a new scroll
- The day changes (resets to empty stamps)

### Widget Sizes

**Small Widget (2×2)**
- Streak at top
- 3 stamps at bottom
- Minimalist and clean

**Medium Widget (4×2)**  
- Full scroll information
- Streak in top right
- 3 stamps in bottom right
- Shows scroll title and progress

**Lock Screen - Circular**
- Just your streak number
- Fits in the row of circles below time

**Lock Screen - Rectangular**
- App name
- Streak
- Three dots for stamps (• • •)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Widget Not Showing Up
- Make sure you've used the app at least once
- Try force-quitting and reopening the app
- Remove and re-add the widget

### Journal Widget Shows "No entries"
- Write at least one journal entry in the app
- Make sure the entry is **published** (not a draft)
- To publish a draft: open it, then tap the ✓ checkmark button

### Widget Not Updating
1. Open the Ten Scrolls app
2. Make a small change (like viewing a scroll)
3. The widget should refresh within a few seconds

If still not working:
- Check **Settings → Ten Scrolls → Background App Refresh** is ON
- Remove and re-add the widget
- Restart your iPhone

### Stamps Not Changing Color
- Complete a reading session in the app
- Make sure you're reading during the correct time window:
  - Dawn: 5am-11am
  - Midday: 11am-4pm
  - Dusk: 4pm-11pm
- Widget should update within 10 seconds

## 📊 Widget Privacy

**What's Shown:**
- ✅ Streak count (just a number)
- ✅ Reading stamps (symbols, no personal info)
- ✅ Scroll numbers and titles (generic practice names)
- ✅ Journal entries (your words, but you chose to write them)

**What's NOT Shown:**
- ❌ Specific habits you're tracking
- ❌ Detailed logs or statistics  
- ❌ Draft journal entries (only published)
- ❌ Passwords or sensitive data

**Privacy Tips:**
- Use **Small** widgets for more discretion in public
- **Large** journal widgets show more text (less private)
- **Lock screen** widgets are visible to anyone who can see your phone
- You can always remove widgets temporarily if needed

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I choose which journal entry the widget shows?**
A: Not directly - it rotates automatically. But you can long press → Refresh to see a different one immediately.

**Q: How often does the journal widget change?**
A: Every 2 hours automatically. You can also manually refresh anytime.

**Q: Can I have multiple widgets?**
A: Yes! Add as many as you want. You can have multiple journal widgets showing different entries (refresh at different times).

**Q: Do widgets drain battery?**
A: No - they're designed to be extremely efficient. Updates happen in the background with minimal power use.

**Q: Can I put widgets on iPad?**
A: If the app supports iPad, yes! Same process as iPhone.

**Q: What if I delete a journal entry?**
A: If the widget is currently showing that entry, it will switch to a different one on the next refresh.

**Q: Do I need internet for widgets?**
A: No - widgets work completely offline. They pull data from the app stored on your device.

**Q: Can I tap the widget to open the app?**
A: Yes! Tapping any widget opens the Ten Scrolls app directly.
